Roast Lamb

Serves - 6 :: Prep Time - 20 min :: Roast time - 90 min

Roast lamb recipe that uses rosemary and garlic to season. The roast beef should turn out juicy, tender, rich in smells and with mouth watering flavours.

Great with gravy and mash potatoes!

Ingredients

  • 2kg leg of lamb
  • 3 garlic cloves, halved
  • 2 tbsp fresh rosemary
  • Salt & black pepper

Intructions

  1. Prepare the Weber BBQ for indirect cooking over medium heat (190°C to 230°C). Using a Weber BBQ, set up your Weber BBQ with a convection tray and trivet.
  2. Using a sharp knife, cut 10 to 12 small slits into the flesh of the lamb. Push a piece of garlic and a small sprig of rosemary into each slit.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. To estimate the cooking time, measure the meat through its thickest part and cook for 1 minute per millimetre for medium. For example if the roast is 90mm thick, cook for 90 minutes.
  5. Roast the lamb over indirect medium heat for 1 ¼ to 1 ½ hours, with the lid closed, or until cooked to your liking.
  6. Once the roast lamb has cooked, remove from the Weber BBQ and leave to rest for 15 minutes before carving.
